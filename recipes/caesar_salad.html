Caeser Salad

Quick Homemade Caeser Salad

Ingredients:

Dressing

  • 1 large egg
  • 45g Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 can (45g) Anchovies
  • 1 clove Garlic
  • 50g lemon juice
  • 10g water
  • 275g neutral oil (I like a mild Olive oil)

Bread Crumbs

  • 50g Olive oil
  • 150g Panko bread crumbs

Little Gem Lettuce

Capers

Chicken
