Caeser Salad

Quick Homemade Caeser Salad

As a big fan of Caesar Salads I always order when I see one I haven't tried. I've made this recipe several times and it's up towards the top. I learned it from Brian Lagerstrom's YouTube video.

Ingredients:

Dressing

  • 1 large egg
  • 45g Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 can (45g) Anchovies (rinse well with water)
  • 1 clove Garlic
  • 50g lemon juice
  • 10g water
  • 275g neutral oil (I like a mild Olive oil)

Bread Crumbs

  • 50g Olive oil
  • 150g Panko bread crumbs

Little Gem Lettuce

  • 4 heads

Capers

  • 20g chopped

Chicken

  • 4 tenders
