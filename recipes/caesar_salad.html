Caeser Salad

Quick Homemade Caeser Salad

As a big fan of Caesar Salads I always order when I see one I haven't tried. I've made this recipe several times and it's up towards the top. I learned it from Brian Lagerstrom's YouTube video.

Ingredients:

Dressing

  • 1 large egg
  • 45g Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 can (45g) Anchovies (rinse well with water)
  • 1 clove Garlic
  • 50g lemon juice
  • 10g water
  • 275g neutral oil (I like a mild Olive oil)

Bread Crumbs

  • 50g Olive oil
  • 150g Panko bread crumbs

Chicken

  • 4 tenders

Little Gem Lettuce

  • 4 heads

Capers

  • 20g chopped

Steps:

    Dressing
  1. Add dressing ingredients to large cup except oil.
  2. Blend well with imersion blender.
  3. Add oil by slowly streaming into cup while blending.
  4. After mixed, set finished dressing in refridgerator.
    Bread Crumbs
  1. In large hot skillet over medium high heat, add olive oil.
  2. Add Panko bread crumbs to hot oil and toss until golden brown.
  3. Place onto paper towel to pull away excess oil and set aside.
  4. Wipe used skillet for use with chicken.
    Little Gem Lettuce
  1. Cut each head into 3 pieces
  2. Rinse well and dry with salad spinner
  3. Set in refridgerator
    Chicken
  1. Heat used skillet over medium, coat with olive oil, add chicken after seasoning.
  2. Cover and sear for 5 minutes.
  3. Flip, cover and turn heat to low, sear for additional 2-3 minutes.
  4. Remove when internal temperture reaches 145 FÂ°.
  5. Cut into bite size peices.
  6. Or skip cooking the chicken and buy on from Costco like I do and add it in.
